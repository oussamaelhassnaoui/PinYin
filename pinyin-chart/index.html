Frequently Asked Questions about the Pinyin Chart

How do I use a Pinyin chart effectively?

To use a Pinyin chart effectively, practice one row or column at a time. Listen to the audio for each sound (if available) and try to mimic it. Combine initials and finals to form syllables and practice them with all four tones.

Why are some cells in the Pinyin chart empty?

Some cells in a standard Pinyin chart are empty because not all initial and final combinations form valid syllables in Mandarin Chinese. For example, the combination 'b' and 'o' (bo) is common, but 'b' and '√º' (b√º) does not exist.
